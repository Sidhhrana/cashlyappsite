Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Your data stays on your device

Cashly is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that stores all your financial data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal or financial information on external servers.

What we collect

We collect nothing. There is no account, no sign-up, and no backend database. All data you enter (transactions, budgets, wallets, savings goals) is stored entirely in your browser's local storage.

Analytics

Cashly does not use any third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising SDKs. There are no cookies that track you across websites.

Exports

When you export your data as a CSV, the file is generated locally on your device and is never sent to any server.
